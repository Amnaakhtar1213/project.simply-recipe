Chicken Biryani

Firstly take a 1Kg chicken.Wash it throughly. Marinate it with yogurt, salt, red chilli, turmeric, lemons, food colour, ginger-garlic paste, and more spices to enhance flavours. On the Other side take 1Kg basmati Rice, wash it 2,3 time with clean water. Now take a big pot filled with water.Add some salt for taste. On the other hand add some oil in the pot and then the marinated chicken and cook it well. After boiling rice and cook chicken well. Now take another pot, In this stage add layers of rice and meat, one layer of rice and one layer of meat. Then cover the pot and seal it for 10 min.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Salad breakfast Lunch Dinner

instructions

step 1

1Kg meat and marinate it with salt, red chilli, yogurt and other spices.

step 2

Take 1kg basmati rice and wash it 2, 3 time with clean water.

step 3

Boil the rice and cook the marinated chicken in oil. after cooking it well. Add layers of rice and chicken one by one and cover the pot for 15 min on low flame.

ingredients

1Kg chicken

1Kg Basmati rice

other spices for marination

tools

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
